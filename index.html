FAQ

Answers, without the fluff

Short and clear.

How does the free example work?
You tell us your trade and location. We create a sample layout and copy so you can see what your site could look like. If you like it, we move forward.
How fast can we go live?
Once you approve the design and provide the basics (services, area, contact details, and any photos), we can usually launch quickly.
Is there a contract?
No. There’s no long contract. Keep it if it’s working for you.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime. Simple as that.
Do you handle hosting and updates?
Yes. Hosting, maintenance, and content updates are handled by Buildify as part of the monthly plan.
What if I already have a website?
We can rebuild it into a cleaner, call-focused single-page site, or keep what works and simplify what doesn’t.
What do you need from me to start?
Your trade, service area, phone/email, a short list of services, and any photos you want to use. That’s it.
